Our Identity

ROCK.SCOT was forged from a simple realization: the West of Scotland is the heartbeat of the nation’s music scene, yet it lacks a dedicated, high-fidelity platform that prioritizes local resonance over generic broadcasting.

We aren't just a radio station; we are a digital-first ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between emerging local talent and an audience that demands more than just background noise. From the docks of Inverclyde to the coasts of Ayrshire and the industrial core of the Central Belt, we are the frequency of the region.
