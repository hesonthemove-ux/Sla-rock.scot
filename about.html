Loud. Local. Scottish.

ROCK.SCOT is the definitive voice for rock music across Central Scotland. We aren't just a radio station; we are a community dedicated to the riffs, the anthems, and the unsigned talent that makes the Scottish music scene world-class.

Our Mission

From the heart of Glasgow to the venues of Edinburgh and everywhere in between, we provide a platform for the music that matters. Whether it’s classic legends or the next big thing from a basement in Paisley, if it rocks, we play it.

Supporting the Scene

We believe in more than just airplay. ROCK.SCOT actively supports local venues, independent festivals, and the technical crews that keep the lights on. We are committed to regional expansion, ensuring every corner of Central Scotland has a station that speaks their language.
