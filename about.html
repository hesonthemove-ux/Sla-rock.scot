Central Scotland Rock

We don’t do background noise. We do rock music. All of it. From the legends who built the scene to the bands currently tearing up the basement bars.

ROCK.SCOT is built on a simple foundation: Serving the public and feeding the riff. We are a dedicated regional asset for Central Scotland, delivering a high-voltage stream that covers every corner of the genre. If it’s got grit and it’s got heart, it’s on the air.

All Rock. No Limits.

Classic, Alt, Metal, Punk, or Grunge. We don’t care about labels; we care about the energy. We broadcast the full spectrum of the loud.

Unsigned & Raw

Why we're here

The scene in Central Scotland is alive, and it deserves a station that’s as confident as the music it plays. We aren't here to play it safe. We are here to support the local venues, back the artists who are grinding, and give the public a rock station that actually belongs to them.
