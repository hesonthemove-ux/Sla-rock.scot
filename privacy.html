Privacy Policy

Data Sovereignty & Governance

At ROCK.SCOT, we prioritize the integrity of the data provided by our listeners, artists, and commercial partners. This policy outlines our commitment to transparency in accordance with 2026 UK GDPR standards.

1. Data Collection

We collect information specifically through our Artist Submission and Commercial Engagement portals. This includes:

  • Identity Markers: Name, Band Identity, or Organization name.
  • Contact Vectors: Email addresses and regional location within Central Scotland.
  • Digital Assets: URL links to music or creative portfolios.

2. Purpose of Processing

Data is processed strictly for the facilitation of regional broadcasting. We utilize your information to:

  • Evaluate musical talent for airplay architecture.
  • Facilitate commercial airtime transactions (£299/£449 tiers).
  • Respond to direct identity and branding inquiries.

3. Third-Party Protocols

ROCK.SCOT does not engage in the sale of user data. We do not share your private contact information with "Wankernomics" style data brokers. Data is only shared with essential service providers (e.g., our streaming host) to maintain broadcast continuity.

4. Your Sovereignty

Under current legislation, you have the right to request the deletion of your data from our architecture. Should you wish to be removed from our talent or commercial databases, contact us at HQ@ROCK.SCOT.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We utilize minimal, non-intrusive cookies to ensure the stability of the Single Page Application (SPA) environment and to track regional listener density for our commercial advertisers.
