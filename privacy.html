PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Data Collection

When you use the ROCK.SCOT app or website, we collect minimal data. Our audio stream provider (FastCast4u) receives your IP address solely to deliver the audio and track general listener numbers. This data is anonymised and not sold to third parties.

2. App Features

Our Android and iOS apps may monitor your "Phone State" (Idle, In-Call). This is used strictly to pause the music when you receive a phone call. We do not record audio, access your contacts, or track your physical location beyond general region detection for the weather widget.

3. Cookies

We use essential cookies to remember your player volume and to provide local weather data for the West of Scotland.

4. Contact Us

For any data requests, contact our Data Officer at studio@rock.scot.
